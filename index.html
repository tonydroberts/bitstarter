FAQ

Is there water on Europa?
Yes, Europa has a surface of water 100 km thick, composed of an icy crust with a liquid ocean beneath.
Is there oxygen on Europa?
Yes! Europa's atmosphere is mostly oxygen, at a pressure of one trillionth of an Earth atmosphere.
